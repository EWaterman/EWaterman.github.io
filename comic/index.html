What if only the rich had super powers?

Following a recent and devastating financial crisis, America struggles to cope with record high levels of unemployment. Hit hardest are the nation’s non-powered communities, which happen to be composed primarily of minorities.

With citizens in desperate need of financial support and with the government’s unwillingness and inability to provide it, conflict is unavoidable.

As tensions continue to rise, the ethnically diverse team of heroes is forced to choose sides in a morally grey struggle that will decide the future of the entire country.
