About Me


Hello! I'm Evan Waterman, a full stack developer currently studying Computer Science at the University of British Columbia.

When it comes to code, I have a particular passion for three things:

  • Writing clear, concise code that can easily be picked up by newcomers to a project.
  • Designing with unit functionality to create a simple, adaptable framework.
  • Optimization! Be efficient and have scalability in mind.


Outside of my studies, I like to play video games (Star Wars KoTOR, The Talos Principle, Deus Ex), read comics (Watchmen, Y the Last Man, Scott Pilgrim) and think about hard questions (like the meaning of life and stuff).
